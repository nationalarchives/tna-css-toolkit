Default blockquote

Examples

The quote appears indented within an article with a light grey opening quotation mark in the background. The quote should be a single paragraph with the provider of the quote applied using the Attribution function. The quote provider’s will then appear to the bottom right of the quote at a smaller size.

Source Title

Usage

	<p>The quote appears...</p>
